Mission

Our mission is to bring together longtime soccer fans and educate new ones by building an engaged and interconnected community supporting the beautiful game. We work with all sides of soccer through offerings ranging from social to analytical to business.

National Organization

The Collegiate Soccer Society is a national organization with chapters at over 15 colleges across the United States. Individual chapters come together to take part in fundraisers, speaker events with major figures in the soccer community, and nationwide FIFA tournaments. National leadership will guide chapters as the one-stop-shop for everything and anything soccer.

Local Organizations

On campus, the Collegiate Soccer Society is a club for soccer fans to come together through events including match viewings, pickup soccer, FIFA, and more!

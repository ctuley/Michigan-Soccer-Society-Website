Welcome to MSS

We are a proudly run student organization at the University of Michigan passionate about soccer and analytics. Our mission is to allow members to combine their passions for soccer and statistics through weekly meetings, presentations, group discussions, and hands-on projects. Above all, we want to create a platform for students to engage with the evolving industry of soccer and data analytics. Check back to this website regularly to find new articles on analytics,

tactics, and anything else related to the beautiful game! Make sure to follow us on Twitter @msocanalytics and connect with us on LinkedIn!

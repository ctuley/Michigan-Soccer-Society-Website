Welcome to the Michigan Soccer Society!

We are a voluntary student organization at the University of Michigan (Ann Arbor) for those passionate about the world of soccer. Our mission is to increase engagement with and appreciation for the beautiful game by building a community of soccer fans on campus. MSS hosts speaker events, match viewings, FIFA tournaments, intramural soccer, and more! Our new analytics division allows students to combine their interest in data science with their love for soccer by publishing articles using data and discussing football through an analytical lens.

Check back to this website regularly for club updates, event details, and original articles on the beautiful game—and don’t forget to subscribe to our newsletter!

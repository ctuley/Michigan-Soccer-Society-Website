Analytics



Formerly the Michigan Soccer Analytics Society (M-SOC), M-SOC decided to merge with the Michigan Soccer Society (MSS) for the love of the game. MSS with M-SOC is a one stop shop for the soccer loving community at the University of Michigan. Here, on this page, you'll find data analysis of players, games, and more. Check back later for projects!

MSS's Analytics Branch is dedicated to combine our passion for soccer and data through weekly meetings, presentations, group discussions, and hands-on projects. Above all, we want to create a platform for students to engage with the evolving industry of soccer and data analytics.

Follow @msocanalytics on Twitter for daily analysis of all things soccer!
